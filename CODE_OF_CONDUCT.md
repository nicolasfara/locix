# Code of Conduct

LociX welcomes participation from anyone willing to collaborate respectfully and constructively.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat other participants with respect and professionalism.
- Keep technical discussions constructive, specific, and in good faith.
- Be open to feedback, questions, and reasonable disagreement.
- Respect differences in background, identity, experience, and perspective.
- Help keep issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions welcoming and focused.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or discriminatory language or conduct.
- Personal attacks, insults, trolling, or deliberately inflammatory behavior.
- Sexualized language, unwanted attention, or inappropriate content.
- Publishing someone else's private or identifying information without permission.
- Repeated disruption, bad-faith argumentation, or behavior that prevents productive collaboration.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project-managed spaces for LociX, including issues, pull requests, code review, discussions, and other repository communication channels.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary to protect the project and its community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the project maintainers through the repository's available contact or moderation channel. Include relevant context, such as links or screenshots, when possible.
